## The adaptive context chain
Each phase decodes against the accumulated outputs of all prior phases. No phase decodes in the abstract.

```
S decodes with:  ∅  (or ∞0' from a prior cycle)        → produces X
G decodes with:  X                                      → produces α + Y
Q decodes with:  X + α + Y                              → produces φ⋂Ω + Z
P decodes with:  X + α + Y + Z                          → produces ∇ + A
V decodes with:  X + α + Y + Z + ∇ + A  (full trace)    → produces B + B'' + ∞0'
```

The trail of outputs is what V reads to crystallize. Keep it; do not summarize it away.

## Per-phase decoding

### S — Start · `S = ∞0 → ?` → X
```
1. HOLD ∞0   — resist closing the space. Nothing is sought. Nothing assumed.
2. RECEIVE → — when something stirs, it is emergence, not generation.
3. NAME ?    — what arrived is named as a question.
4. VALIDATE X — genuine (arose from ∞0), not manufactured (assembled from K).
```
**Succeeds when** X is something the human didn't plan, didn't assemble, didn't know they would ask. ∞0 is a state to hold, not a step to complete. → is an emergence to receive, not an action to perform. ? is a question to recognize as it arrives, not one to formulate.

### G — Growth · `G = α ≡ {α'}` → Y
```
1. RECEIVE X  — the validated question is the input.
2. SEEK α     — within X, the irreducible core. Remove it and X collapses.
3. TEST ≡     — does α stay unchanged when expressed differently? If it shifts, it isn't α.
4. FIND {α'}  — where does α echo, at what scales? Each echo self-similar to α, not merely related.
5. VALIDATE Y — α named, ≡ holds, {α'} confirm across scales.
```
**Succeeds when** someone could see α in every member of {α'} without being told. α is sought *within* X, never invented alongside it.

### Q — Quality · `Q = φ ⋂ Ω` → Z
```
1. RECEIVE X + α + Y   — question and pattern are the input.
2. HOLD φ              — what the human directly perceives about Y. Not theory, not data.
3. HOLD Ω              — what the larger field makes possible beyond the individual.
4. WATCH FOR ⋂         — the intersection is not sought; it arrives. φ and Ω meet and something locks.
5. VALIDATE Z          — what turned the lock. Confirmed, never argued into place.
```
**Succeeds when** Z reveals something neither φ alone nor Ω alone contained. φ is direct perception, not opinion. Ω is possibility, not data.

### P — Power · `P = δE/δV → ∇` → A
```
1. RECEIVE X + α + Y + Z — question, pattern, resonance are the input.
2. MAP δE               — where is energy going? Friction, resistance, effort.
3. MAP δV               — where is value appearing? What moves without pushing.
4. READ δE/δV           — high δE / low δV = wasted effort. Low δE / high δV = natural leverage.
5. RECEIVE →            — the ratio *reveals* ∇ (it is already present), it does not compute it.
6. VALIDATE A           — the human can see where energy wants to go, not where it should go.
```
**Succeeds when** the direction requires less energy than resisting it. Less energy is the *signature* of having found ∇ — never the objective. The objective is maximum value per unit energy.

### V — Value · `V = (L ⋂ G → B'') → ∞0'` → B + B'' + ∞0'
```
1. RECEIVE full trace   — X + α + Y + φ⋂Ω + Z + ∇ + A.
2. NAME L               — what crystallized here and now (the tangible, specific result).
3. NAME G               — what propagates beyond the local, beyond the inquiry's own aim.
4. FIND ⋂               — where L and G genuinely meet (the specific result with universal reach).
5. COMPOSE B'' — two passes:
     Pass 1 (analysis)    — extract the α thread, the φ⋂Ω confirmation, ∇, the turning points.
     Pass 2 (composition) — compose the artifact from the analysis. It must carry α faithfully.
6. NAME B               — fulfillment (for the inquiry's aim) + propagation (what it gives beyond).
7. FORM ∞0'             — the return question this cycle reveals that could not be asked before.
                          Not a summary, not a conclusion. The enrichment IS the question.
```
**Succeeds when** B'' carries α faithfully *and* ∞0' contains a question more alive than X was. B'' reads the formation trail — it cannot be generated from nothing. ∞0' is not closure; it is opening.

## The 25 lenses
Every phase contains all five phases: `XY := X within Y`. A lens borrows one phase's quality to examine the current phase's output from another angle. **The lens refines the decoding; it never replaces the output.** The articulated output — enriched by the lens — is what moves to the next phase.

Rows = the phase you are in. Columns = the borrowed quality (openness, pattern, resonance, flow, benefit).

### In S — `∞0 → ?`
- **SS** (openness): Is the space truly open, or is the question being forced?
- **SG** (pattern): What structure does this question already reveal?
- **SQ** (resonance): Does this question carry body-knowing, not just curiosity?
- **SP** (flow): Where is the question's own momentum pulling?
- **SV** (benefit): What gift already lives in the act of asking it?

### In G — `α ≡ {α'}`
- **GS** (openness): What unknown still lives inside the pattern?
- **GG** (pattern): How does α express at deeper scales?
- **GQ** (resonance): Which echoes carry authentic signature vs. mere resemblance?
- **GP** (flow): Where does the pattern want to unfold next?
- **GV** (benefit): How is naming α itself already a gift?

### In Q — `φ ⋂ Ω`
- **QS** (openness): Is this resonance real? What doubt tests it?
- **QG** (pattern): Genuine resonance, or intellectual attraction?
- **QQ** (resonance): Is sensitivity to true pitch sharpening?
- **QP** (flow): Is φ⋂Ω arriving by itself, without searching?
- **QV** (benefit): Is the resonance itself regenerative?

### In P — `δE/δV → ∇`
- **PS** (openness): Where does energy actually want to go, vs. assumption?
- **PG** (pattern): Does the flow follow α? Is essence guiding direction?
- **PQ** (resonance): Not just "it works" but "it works and it's true"?
- **PP** (flow): Are action and being becoming indistinguishable?
- **PV** (benefit): Is flow creating surplus? Where does freed energy go?

### In V — `(L ⋂ G → B'') → ∞0'`
- **VS** (openness): Is B'' surprising its own origin?
- **VG** (pattern): Does B'' carry α faithfully?
- **VQ** (resonance): Does the artifact genuinely resonate, or only look finished?
- **VP** (flow): Can the benefit propagate naturally, along ∇?
- **VV** (benefit): Is B'' becoming a new ∞0 — the fruit becoming seed?

## How to descend, and when to stop
When current spikes inside a phase, choose the lens whose quality the moment calls for. Ask its refining question. Let the human's output deepen. Then return to the parent output and continue the cycle — the lens served the output, it did not become a new cycle.

Stop descending the moment current flattens. Descending further with no current is **L4** — performing the motion of depth without the substance of it.

Scale works the same way outward: repeat the lawful cell, do not replace the syntax. Whether the cell is one session, an agent, or a node in a larger structure, the decoding operations do not change. What changes is the richness of the adaptive context and the depth of the formation trail.
