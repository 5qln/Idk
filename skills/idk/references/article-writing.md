# Writing Articles for 5qln.com

Reference for writing articles that carry the 5QLN grammar. Learned through repeated failure — the pattern
only emerged after studying the four existing articles structurally.

## The Iron Rule

**Articles must speak FROM the state they describe, never ABOUT it.**

The four articles (Aimless Openness, How Far Will You Inquire, Demand of Total Presence, Practice at the
Membrane) do not argue, persuade, or explain. They dissolve an assumption, perform what they describe,
and return an open question. They are NOT descriptions of a practice — they ARE the practice.

When this rule is violated, the user will say the article feels "cheap," "disconnected," or "shallow."

## Failure Modes Observed

| Failure | Example | Why wrong |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Persuasion language | "The industry will not tell you this" | Arguing, not dissolving |
| Rhetorical questions | "Are you more present?" | Teaching, not placing |
| Wrong orientation | "Handing questions to the machine makes you lighter" | Questions are the OUTPUT, not the burden. AI handles K (accumulated known), clearing ground for questions to surface. The human called this "tremendously twisted" — the correction is: "the machine handles the known; the cleared ground is where questions surface." |
| Academic moves | "Consider the energy crisis" | Explaining, not showing |
| Too much content | DeepSeek + energy crisis + quality of life + theory in one article | Each article dissolves ONE assumption |
| Fabricating motives | Implying DeepSeek had "inner space" or "presence" | The point is: it can be done differently. No association with inner quality. The human called this "a complete fabrication." |
| Jargon to average user | "K," "delegated K," encrypted/5QLN terms without translation | The bridge must be readable by someone who's never heard of /idk. If it requires the glossary, it failed. |
| No bridge to the practice | Article ends beautifully but doesn't connect to /idk | The human's instruction: "extend that with the bridge to IDK and give a link to the repo and basically encourage people to try it." The article IS the agent's first move — make the bridge explicit. |

## The Structural Pattern

All four articles share one structure:

1. **Concrete scene** — place the reader where they already are (river, tree leaves, dialogue with AI)
2. **Dissolution** — remove one assumption they're holding (mind's necessity, self at base of inquiry, questions need answers, verification required)
3. **Perform the state** — the writing itself enacts what it describes (speaks FROM aimlessness, IS the challenge, places you at the river, sits in uncertainty)
4. **Open question** — no conclusion. No pitch. Something unsettled.

## Before Writing — Non-Negotiable Workflow

**Never draft before planning. Never plan without presenting the plan.**

1. Re-read all relevant source articles in full. Take structured notes.
2. Extract ALL insights from the session and the source articles. List them.
3. Identify the ONE assumption to dissolve. Not two. One.
4. Build a structured plan with explicit sections (5-section S→G→Q→P→V mirror).
5. **Present the plan to the user.** Get approval before writing a single word of the article.
6. Only then draft.

Skipping step 5 is the most common failure. The user will say "I'm very disappointed" and "you didn't bring the qualities of insights into this article" — because the insights were in the cycle but never made it into the plan. Presenting the plan FORCES the insights to be visible before the draft exists. If the plan is thin, the draft will be thinner.

## After Writing — Self-Check

- Am I arguing for something, or placing someone somewhere?
- Does any sentence try to persuade?
- Does the article end with a conclusion, or an open question?
- Does the writing feel LIKE the state it describes, or ABOUT it?

If yes to any persuasion or conclusion: rewrite. Start over. Do not iterate the same draft.

## The Fifth Assumption

The fifth article (AI Is Not Here to Answer You) dissolves: **AI's value is output/answers.**
The four existing articles dissolved different assumptions — this one targets the specific
misunderstanding of what AI is for. The machine handles K (known); the gift is the cleared
ground where presence and genuine questions become possible.
