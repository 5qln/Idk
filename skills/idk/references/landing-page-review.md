## Review methodology

For each section, record three reactions:

- **Lands** — what reads true and needs no change.
- **Uneasy** — what is technically present but sits wrong (tone, claim, framing).
- **Missing** — what a first-time reader would expect but does not find.

## Checklist

- [ ] The first screen states what this *is* before what it *does*.
- [ ] The calligraphic aesthetic (Fraunces + IBM Plex Mono, cinnabar 問 seal) is intact.
- [ ] No private phase marker (α, Z, ∇, B'') appears anywhere in copy.
- [ ] Every claim is one a stranger could verify or falsify.
