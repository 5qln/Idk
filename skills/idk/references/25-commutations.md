# 25 Commutations — Sub-Phase Fractal Decode

> Status: scaffold. Reference cited from `SKILL.md` (Commutation mood). Fill in
> from the canonical 5×5 grid when promoting to a sealed surface.

Post-hoc verification applied to a **completed** cycle's output — never run
during the live cycle. Each of the five phases (S·G·Q·P·V) contains all five
sub-phases, giving a 5×5 grid from **S·s** through **V·v**. Expanding a finished
design or artifact through the grid checks structural soundness at every fractal
level.

Use when the human asks to "decode in sub-phase mode" or "run the 25
commutations of the fractal."
